# Reports & dashboard

## Saving a report

Ask your agent to save its findings:

> "Save this as a report titled 'Q3 revenue review' in the Q3 folder."

The agent composes a markdown report — the findings **together with** their trust levels and
caveats — and stores it under your account. Only the report you choose to save leaves your machine
(see [Privacy](/docs/privacy)); your raw data never does.

## Folders

Group related reports:

> "Create a folder called 'Client — Acme'."

Then save reports into it. Browse folders on your [dashboard](/dashboard).

## The dashboard

At [/dashboard](/dashboard) you can:

- see your plan and upgrade,
- browse folders and reports,
- open any report to read it,
- **share** a report as a public link.

## Sharing

Open a report and click **Share** to mint a public URL (`/r/<token>`). Anyone with the link can
read that one report — no account needed. Turn sharing off at any time to revoke the link.
