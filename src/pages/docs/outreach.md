# Outreach connector

Turn Table Intelligence into an AI outreach assistant: your agent researches prospects, drafts
personalised emails, saves them to your account for review, and — once you approve — sends them
through your own email provider. The dashboard is where you see and edit everything; the AI does
the work.

> Outreach is a **premium connector**. Start a trial or upgrade on [pricing](/pricing).

## 1. Connect a sending account

Outreach sends from **your own email**, so replies come to you and your domain reputation stays
yours. On the [outreach dashboard](/dashboard/outreach), add:

- your **from address** (an address on a domain you've verified in Resend),
- an optional **from name**,
- your **Resend API key**.

Your key is stored on your account and used only to send — it's never shown in the browser again.

## 2. Create an outreach prompt

An outreach prompt is your editable playbook — the tone, structure, and rules the agent follows
when drafting. Keep several for different campaigns:

> "Create an outreach prompt called 'Agencies — warm intro' that opens with one specific line
> about the prospect and keeps the pitch to three sentences."

## 3. Research & draft

Point your agent at a list or a description, and let it work:

> "Find 20 SaaS founders in Singapore, research each, and draft outreach using the
> 'Agencies — warm intro' prompt. Save them for me to review."

The prospects — name, email, company, research, and the drafted email — appear on your
[dashboard](/dashboard/outreach), each marked **drafted**.

## 4. Review, then send

Edit anything in the dashboard. When you're happy:

> "Send the email to Maya at Acme."  — or —  "Send all the drafted prospects."

Status updates to **sent**. To capture replies, ask your agent to check your inbox (via your email
tool) and record them:

> "Check for replies and mark the ones who responded."

## What's stored where

Prospects, prompts, and send status live on your Table Intelligence account so the dashboard can
show them. Your raw analytics data stays on your machine (see [Privacy](/docs/privacy)). Your
email key is used only to send on your behalf.
